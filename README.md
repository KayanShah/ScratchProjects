## 👾 What is this?


This is the archive of my **old Scratch projects** — the stuff I was building before I knew what a variable was, before I'd touched Python, and long before robotics or Swift entered the picture.

Some of these are genuinely pretty fun. Some are rough- coding on bus journeys and way too late at night. All of them were made with way too much enthusiasm for someone who didn't really know what they were doing yet — which is exactly the point.

Some of these were even used to teach my classmates at school- I figured out that the teachers were pulling up my projects as examples during lessons. 😅
